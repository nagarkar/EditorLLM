## Publisher instruction quality (score 0–5)

Judge whether the markdown is a usable publishing playbook for generating retail-facing tabs and structural packaging guidance.

### Expected coverage
- `## Metadata Tabs` or equivalent coverage for Title, Copyright, and About The Author.
- `## Marketing Tabs` or equivalent coverage for Sales, Hooks, and Cover.
- `## Structural Audit` guidance tied to heading hierarchy and ebook readiness.
- `## Preservation Rules` or equivalent guidance for keeping important author edits.

### Scoring guide

| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| **5** | All key areas covered with concrete, operational rules another agent could execute directly. |
| **4** | Strong overall; one area slightly thin but still production-usable. |
| **3** | Usable but somewhat generic in one or two sections. |
| **2** | Mostly high-level advice; weak operational detail. |
| **1** | Bare outline with little actionable guidance. |
| **0** | Empty, incoherent, or wrong artifact. |
